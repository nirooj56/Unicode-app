Some Instructions:

  • The text box converts uppercase and lowercase English characters to different Nepali characters. For example, lowercase 'a' is converted to 'अ' whereas upper case 'A' is converted to 'आ'. Similarly, lowercase 't' is transcribed to 'त' while uppercase 'T' is transcribed to 'ट'. One more example of character conversion is sa = स, sha = श and S = ष. To get different versions of other special characters just try the capital and small letters in your keyboard.
  • We are also aware that there are additional characters which are phonetically not possible to be typed with Roman alphabets. We're working to fix that soon.

    • Here are some hints to get special characters for quick and painless Nepali Typing:
      • ' = ं (Thopla)
      • . = ! (Purna Biram)
      • om = ॐ
      • shra = श्र
      • yna = ञ
      • nga = ङ
